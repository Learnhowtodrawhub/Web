About This Lesson

Welcome to the course! We are so excited to have you join our drawing community. In this first lesson, we'll quickly go over what you can expect from the course, and what basic supplies we recommend for getting the most out of our tutorials.

Required Supplies:

  • Pencil: A standard No. 2 pencil is perfect.
  • Eraser: A kneaded or vinyl eraser works best for cartoons.
  • Paper: Standard printer paper or a simple sketchbook.
  • Markers/Coloring Medium: Anything from crayons to colored pencils, based on your preference!

Next Steps:

Once you have your materials, click on **1.2 The Basic Shapes of Life** in the left sidebar to start drawing!
